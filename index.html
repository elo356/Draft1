Mission & Vision

Building Puerto Rico's future with excellence and integrity

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality construction and project management services in Puerto Rico by combining local expertise, operational discipline, and a commitment to efficiency, safety, and accountability. We strive to execute every project with precision while building long-term partnerships with clients, developers, and U.S.-based firms.

Our Vision

To become a leading construction partner in Puerto Rico for large-scale residential, infrastructure, and federally funded projects. Our vision is to operate as a trusted bridge between U.S. companies and local execution, scaling our operations to manage multiple projects with consistency, reliability, and excellence.

Our Services

Comprehensive construction and project management solutions

Construction Services

Complete construction solutions from ground-up projects to finishing work.

  • Ground-up residential construction
  • Structural concrete, foundations, and framing
  • Roofing systems and exterior work
  • Interior finishes (tile, painting, carpentry)

Demolition & Debris Removal

Safe and efficient demolition services with proper disposal.

  • Selective and full structural demolition
  • Interior demolition (kitchens, bathrooms, partitions)
  • Site clearing and debris removal
  • Hauling and disposal coordination
  • Preparation for reconstruction and development

Site Development

Preparing sites for construction with proper groundwork.

  • Land clearing and excavation
  • Soil preparation and compaction
  • Utility trenching and infrastructure prep

Reconstruction & Rehabilitation

Bringing properties back to life with quality reconstruction.

  • Full home reconstruction (including government-funded programs)
  • Structural repairs and upgrades
  • Property rehabilitation for resale or occupancy

Property Repair & Maintenance

Ongoing maintenance and repair services for properties.

  • Property condition assessments
  • Repair scope development and cost estimation
  • General repairs and system upgrades
  • Pre-sale / rent-ready preparation
  • Ongoing property maintenance coordination

Construction & Project Management

End-to-end project oversight and coordination.

  • Project planning and scheduling
  • Budgeting and cost control
  • Subcontractor coordination
  • Quality assurance and site supervision

Government & Federally Funded Projects

We have extensive experience with CDBG-MIT and similar programs, familiarity with FEMA-related processes, and provide documentation, compliance, and reporting support with coordination with municipalities and agencies.

Leadership

Meet the team driving our success

Francisco Cardona

Founder & Managing Director

Francisco Cardona is the Founder and Managing Director of Premier Services, LLC, a Puerto Rico-based construction and project management company. He specializes in residential reconstruction, site development, and the execution of federally funded projects. He holds a Master's degree in Administration, bringing a strong foundation in management, operations, and organizational leadership to project delivery. In addition, his experience as a real estate broker provides valuable insight into property valuation, market conditions, and investment potential in Puerto Rico. Francisco is focused on operational efficiency, cost control, and reliable execution, positioning Premier Services as a trusted partner for developers, contractors, and U.S.-based firms operating in Puerto Rico.

Kiara Rodriguez

Legal & Operations Director

Kiara Rodriguez is the Legal & Operations Director at Premier Services, LLC, where she oversees legal strategy, compliance, and operational support across all projects. She is a licensed attorney in Puerto Rico with experience across the Puerto Rico Court of Appeals, municipalities, government entities, and private practice. Her background includes handling complex legal matters, contract development, and regulatory compliance. Kiara also brings valuable knowledge of the construction sector, supporting project execution through legal oversight, risk management, and structured documentation. Her role strengthens Premier Services' ability to operate efficiently within regulated environments, including government-funded projects.

Jiovan Cardona

Project Manager (On-Site)

Jiovan Cardona serves as the On-Site Project Manager at Premier Services, LLC, overseeing daily field operations and ensuring projects are executed efficiently and according to plan. He is responsible for coordinating crews, managing schedules, and maintaining quality control across all phases of construction. His hands-on approach and field experience play a critical role in keeping projects on track, within budget, and aligned with company standards. Jiovan works closely with leadership and subcontractors to ensure clear communication, accountability, and consistent progress on every job site.
